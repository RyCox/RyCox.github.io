Congratulations!  You've made it to the personal site of Ryan Cox.  This is a simple GitHub pages site created to display my resume, some blog posts, and other somewhat useful information.

Ryan has been working in software development since 2012 when he got his start doing manual sanity testing for Pragmatic Works, a Microsoft business intelligence firm down in Jacksonville.  They allowed him to bang on SSIS packages, tear through SSRS reports, and write pages on pages of documentation about their software.  Because, prior to this, his only experience in the working world was schlepping pizza or selling credit cards for various retail banks, he found this new job much to his liking.

After years of studying automated testing strategies and various web driver frameworks, Ryan found his bread and butter in testing back-end services for Discovery, helping the Shiplap Nation keep the ad revenue rolling in.  Now, he uses java and tools like RestAssured to automate complex microservice interactions to ensure the proverbial lights stay on.

When Ryan gets free time, he's normally having fun with his family in the wilds of Tennessee or, more likely, just being a dad, but every now and then he'll get on here and blog or work on some personal projects on github.

Thanks for visiting!
